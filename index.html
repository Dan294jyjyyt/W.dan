Mission Statement

The Federal Gang & Narcotics Agency (FGNA) is a premier federal law enforcement agency dedicated to dismantling violent and organized criminal enterprises threatening the nation. Our mission is to identify, investigate, and neutralize criminal gangs and drug cartels to protect communities and uphold the rule of law.

Primary Threat Targets

  • RF - Russian Cartel
  • MC Madrazo Cartel
  • Ballas
  • Prophets
  • Deadly Street Gangs

What We Do

FGNA operates through specialized sub-divisions leveraging advanced intelligence, tactical expertise, and interagency collaboration. We conduct surveillance, undercover operations, interdiction, and community outreach, focusing on:

  • Organized crime infiltration and dismantling
  • Narcotics trafficking suppression
  • Violent gang prosecution and disruption
  • Coastal and air interdiction
  • Canine detection and tracking
