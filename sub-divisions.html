Sub-Divisions

ACU — Air Coastal Unit

The Air Coastal Unit provides aerial and maritime support for FGNA operations, extending surveillance and enforcement across coastal zones and inland waterways.

Focus: Aerial reconnaissance & coastal patrol

Operations: Aerial surveillance, waterway interdictions, air support during operations

Personnel: Licensed pilots, drone operators, maritime agents

Lead: None assigned

GIU — Gang Investigation Unit

Leads long-term investigations into organized gang activity to identify, infiltrate, and dismantle criminal networks.

Focus: Gang structure, affiliations, and organized crime

Operations: Undercover ops, gang mapping, evidence collection

Personnel: Case agents, field operatives, intelligence specialists
