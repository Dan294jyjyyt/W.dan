Sub-Divisions

ACU — Air Coastal Unit

The Air Coastal Unit provides aerial and maritime support for FGNA operations, extending surveillance and enforcement capabilities across coastal zones and inland waterways.

Focus: Aerial reconnaissance & coastal patrol

Operations: Aerial surveillance, waterway interdictions, air support during operations

Personnel: Licensed pilots, drone operators, maritime agents

Lead: N/A

GIU — Gang Investigation Unit

The Gang Investigation Unit leads long-term investigative efforts into organized gang activity, working to identify, infiltrate, and dismantle criminal networks.

Focus: Gang structure, affiliations, and organized activity

Operations: Undercover ops, gang mapping, affiliations tracking, evidence collection

Personnel: Case agents, field operatives, intelligence specialists

Lead: J. Price

K9 — Canine Division

The K9 Division provides essential detection and tracking capabilities, supporting narcotics, weapon, and search operations with trained working dogs.

Focus: Detection and tracking

Operations: Drug/bomb detection, suspect tracking, area sweeps

Personnel: Handlers, trainers, canine specialists

Lead: T. Berd

SERT — Special Emergency Response Team

SERT handles high-risk tactical operations, hostage rescues, and specialized counter-gang interventions requiring precision and rapid deployment.

Focus: Tactical response and crisis management

Operations: High-risk warrants, hostage rescue, counter-gang raids

Personnel: Tactical operators, negotiators, crisis responders

Lead: W. Dan
